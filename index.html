Harry Potter

The Harry Potter series, written by J.K. Rowling, traces the coming-of-age journey of Harry Potter,
a boy who learns on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He enrolls at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he not only studies magic but alsouncovers the truth behind his parents’ murder at the hands of the dark wizard Lord Voldemort. As the narrative unfolds over seven books, Harry grapples with identity, loss, and moral complexity while facing Voldemort’s growing threat. The series weaves together themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the enduring conflict between good and evil, set against the backdrop of a richly imagined magical world.
